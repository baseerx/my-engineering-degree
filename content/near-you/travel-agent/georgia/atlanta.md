## **How to Become a Travel Agent in Atlanta**

Aspiring travel agents in Atlanta must be passionate about planning, organizing, and budgeting. They must also have some knowledge about the travel industry and a high school diploma or GED to make admission into training programs easier.

Once they spend a few months or years in training, they will have acquired the necessary skills to secure placement with a host travel agency and start their careers properly. They may also acquire professional credentials from the ***American Society of Travel Advisors*** (ASTA).

Lastly, all travel agents should have an IATA number issued by the ***International Air Transport Association (IATA)***. This gives them access to valuable state-of-the-art billing, planning, ticketing, and continuing education tools.
