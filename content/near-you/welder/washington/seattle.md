## **How to Become a Welder in Seattle**

The city of Seattle does not set any mandatory educational or licensure requirements for welders, nor does the state. However, candidates are expected to complete a training program and become certified by the American Welding Society for better career opportunities.

While looking for the best training destinations, candidates may consider academic and on-the-job training programs. Academic programs are those offered by community colleges and trade schools, while on-the-job training programs are those offered by apprenticeship programs and potential employers.

Fresh welders in Seattle may also choose specific specialty areas such as civil construction and shipbuilding.
